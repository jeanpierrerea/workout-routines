Toji Inspired Routine

Six-Day Workout Plan

Day 1: Back and Shoulders

  1. Deadlifts (Barbell) - 4 sets of 5 reps
  2. Barbell Rows - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  3. Pull-Ups or Assisted Pull-Ups - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  4. Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  5. Lateral Raises - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  6. Face Pulls - 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 2: Legs and Core

  1. Squats (Barbell) - 4 sets of 5 reps
  2. Romanian Deadlifts - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  3. Bulgarian Split Squats - 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
  4. Leg Press - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  5. Plank - 3 sets of 45-60 seconds
  6. Russian Twists - 3 sets of 15-20 reps
